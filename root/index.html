Teams

Mercedes

Mercedes has been a dominantforce in Formula 1 in recent years, winning the constructors' championship every year since 2014. The team is known for its innovative engineering and talented drivers, including Lewis Hamilton, who has won seven world championships with the team.

Mercedes is owned by the German car manufacturer Daimler AG and is based in Brackley, England. The team was originally founded in 1954 as the Mercedes-Benz racing team, and has competed in various forms of motorsport over the years.

Visit Mercedes' website

Red Bull Racing

Red Bull Racing is a team that has made a significant impact in Formula 1 since its debut in 2005. The team has won four constructors' championships and four drivers' championships with Sebastian Vettel between 2010 and 2013.

Red Bull Racing is owned by the energy drinks company Red Bull and is based in Milton Keynes, England. The team is known for its innovative designs and strong focus on aerodynamics.

Visit Red Bull Racing's website

Ferrari

Ferrari is one of the oldest and most successful teams in Formula 1, having competed in every season since the championship began in 1950. The team has won 16 constructors' championships and 15 drivers' championships, making it the most successful team in the history of the sport.

Ferrari is based in Maranello, Italy and is owned by the Italian car manufacturer Fiat Chrysler Automobiles. The team is known for its passionate fans, iconic red livery, and legendary drivers such as Michael Schumacher and Niki Lauda.
